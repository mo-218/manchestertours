Trafford Centre

The Trafford Centre is one of the UK’s largest and most iconic shopping and leisure destinations. With stunning architecture, over 200 stores, restaurants, a cinema, and even a LEGOLAND Discovery Centre, it’s more than just a shopping trip — it’s a full day out for the whole family.

Getting There

Located just outside the city centre, the Trafford Centre is easily accessible via public transport. The Metrolink tram now has a dedicated stop at the Trafford Centre, making travel from the city simple and fast. Multiple bus routes also serve the centre, and it’s just off the M60 motorway if you're driving.
