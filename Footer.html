Visitor Information

Getting Around

Manchester has a highly connected public transport system including buses, trams, and trains. The Metrolink tram is especially useful for getting to key destinations like the Etihad Stadium, Old Trafford, and the Trafford Centre. You can purchase tickets via machines at tram stops or using the Transport for Greater Manchester (TfGM) app.

Weather Tips

Manchester is known for its unpredictable weather. It's always a good idea to carry an umbrella or light waterproof jacket, especially if you're planning to walk or attend an outdoor event. Summers are mild, while winters are damp and chilly.

Staying Safe

Manchester is generally a safe city for visitors, especially in the main tourist areas. Stick to well-lit areas at night and keep your belongings secure in busy places. If you need help, local police and transport staff are friendly and approachable.

Accessibility

Most public attractions, transport, and shopping centres in Manchester are accessible for wheelchair users and those with mobility needs. The Metrolink trams and key stations offer step-free access, and venues like the Trafford Centre offer mobility scooter and wheelchair hire.

Need Help?

For travel updates, visit the Transport for Greater Manchester website. For emergencies, call 999. For non-emergencies, dial 101 to reach local police services.
