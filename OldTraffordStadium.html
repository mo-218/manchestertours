Old Trafford Stadium

Old Trafford, known as the “Theatre of Dreams,” is home to Manchester United Football Club. With a capacity of over 74,000, it’s the largest club football stadium in the UK and steeped in rich history and tradition. A visit to Old Trafford is a pilgrimage for football fans from around the world.

Getting There

Old Trafford is accessible via multiple public transport options. The easiest route is the Metrolink tram to Old Trafford station, just a short walk to the ground. Alternatively, you can take trains to Manchester United Football Ground station or buses running frequently from the city centre. On matchdays, services are increased to accommodate fans.
