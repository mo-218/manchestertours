Etihad Stadium

The Etihad Stadium is home to Manchester City Football Club and one of the most modern stadiums in Europe. Located in the heart of East Manchester, it boasts a capacity of over 53,000 fans and regularly hosts Premier League and international matches. The area around the stadium has been transformed into the Etihad Campus, a thriving sports hub with training facilities and fan attractions.

Getting There

You can reach the Etihad Stadium easily by public transport. The most convenient route is the Metrolink tram, with the Etihad Campus stop just a short walk from the ground. Trams run regularly from Manchester Piccadilly Station and take around 10 minutes. There are also several bus routes and walking paths from the city centre.
